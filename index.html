Ingredient

We are going to make Allo Palak Recipe

  • 1 kg palak
  • 1/2 kg Allo
  • 250 grm oil
  • Green Chilli on need basis
  • 250 grm Tomato
  • 250 grm onion

Method

  1. First of all wash the palak properly and cut it in to small or grind
  2. cut the Allo in small pieces
  3. put the fry pan on the burner
  4. put the oil in frypan and the past of the onion, tomato and chilli in it
  5. mix it proper and keep it on burner for 10 minutes
  6. Then put the allo and palak in it
  7. mix it properly and keep it on burner for 20 minutes
  8. yes the recipe is ready for eat
